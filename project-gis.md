## Earth Observation - Google Earth Engine & EVI

Due to climate change, economic factors, and socio-political factors, food insecurity has become a prominent problem in certain countries.

The goal of this research is to use remote sensing technologies and analyze free accessible satellite data with the help of Artificial Intelligence (AI) to provide farming communities with valuable up-to-date information on vegetation health.
