**10 Years in Oil & Gas industry - Geophysics**

### Exploration, Reservoir Optimization, and Field Development
* Geoscientific studies including Vertical Seismic Profile (VSP), Wireline & LWD VSP, and Fiber Optics.
* Supported operations on well planning/drilling and logging requirements.
* Reviewed, validated, and processed 300+ standard to advanced Borehole Seismic datasets from time picking to corridor stack, 1D/2D images, seismic well tie & carried out/validated post-mortems.
* Interacted regularly with multidisciplinary teams and incorporated specialty studies – log, shear, anisotropy analysis, sonic calibration & synthetic seismogram, Q-Factor, borehole match.

### Modeling and Reservoir Characterization
* 1D/2D/2.5D/3D Modeling, seismic survey, feasibility, and reservoir characterization studies.
* Performed 2D & 3D seismic Interpretation, horizon picking, attribute analyses, interpretation of data Quality Control (QC), and structural mapping.
* Generated and utilized seismic attributes for better mapping structural features.
* Simulated seismic acquisition via ray tracing or finite difference to generate synthetics.

### Field Operations and Monitoring
* Conducted field operations and data acquisition for 5+ projects including Hydraulic Fracture Monitoring (HFM) & Underground Gas Storage for surface and downhole real-time monitoring.
* Conducted pre-job modeling for feasibility studies, performed and reviewed model calibration.
* Implemented and followed Surface and Downhole protocol for monitoring, recalibrating, and recalculating for location and magnitude of microseismic events.

### Leadership and Knowledge Sharing
* Spearheaded geophysics knowledge sharing, coaching, onboarding, and mentoring.
* Onboarded & coached 10+ resources and organized 20+ internal & external publications.
* Acted as an instructor in 10+ training sessions for diverse internal populations up to technical sales and clients.
* Designed unique automated solutions and new workflows to address unique challenges during 100+ highly publicized and heavy workload projects.
